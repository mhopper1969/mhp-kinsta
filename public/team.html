Team

Martyn Hopper – Founder & Managing Director

Martyn is one of the UK's most experienced and trusted regulatory advisers. He brings over 30 years of deep expertise in financial regulation, enforcement, and compliance — spanning public service, private practice, and strategic advisory work at the highest level.

He began his legal career in the UK Government Legal Service, before spending a decade at the Financial Services Authority (FSA), where he played a central role in the development of enforcement policy and regulatory reform.

He then spent more than 21 years as a partner at two of the world's leading law firms — Herbert Smith and Linklaters — both ranked in the top tier of the Legal 500 and Chambers & Partners directories. In both firms, Martyn advised on some of the most complex and sensitive regulatory matters in the UK and globally — including enforcement actions, market conduct, culture reviews, and strategic regulatory issues.

Martyn has been consistently ranked as a top-tier practitioner by both Legal 500 and Chambers for his work in financial services regulatory matters.

He founded Martyn Hopper & Partners to offer clients something different: independent, high-trust advice that combines legal rigour with strategic, operational, and reputational insight. He works with a trusted network of senior professionals to deliver tailored, multidisciplinary advice on the most challenging regulatory questions.
