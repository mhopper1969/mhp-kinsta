About us

Martyn Hopper & Partners is a new kind of advisory firm — created to help clients navigate the legal, strategic, and operational complexity of modern regulation.

We bring together legal expertise, compliance insight, and policy experience to offer multidisciplinary solutions to high-stakes regulatory challenges. Our advice is independent, practical, and rooted in decades of experience in law, enforcement, and institutional leadership.

We work with financial institutions, corporates, start-ups, regulators, governmental bodies, and individual senior executives — advising on both specific regulatory matters and broader systems of governance, accountability, and risk.

Regulated businesses face increasing demands, from both regulators and society. At the same time, public institutions and policymakers face pressure to design frameworks that are not only enforceable and trusted but also support innnvoation and economic growth.

Too often, advice in this space is fragmented: legal, operational, cultural, and reputational issues are treated in isolation. We believe they are interdependent — and that solving regulatory problems requires clear thinking, experience, and a systems-level view.

We created this firm to challenge one-size-fits-all models, reject rote frameworks, and deliver advice that is grounded in how regulation works in practice.

At the heart of our work is a belief in the value of the rule of law — not as an abstract principle, but as the foundation of trust, fairness, and legitimacy in a regulated system.

Our work is also informed by the latest research on regulatory design and effectiveness. We maintain strong relationships with the academic and policy communities to ensure our advice reflects both real-world experience and leading thinking.

We operate differently from traditional law firms and large consultancies:

  • We work with small teams of experienced professionals — not pyramids.
  • We don't measure value by billable hours.
  • We price based on the value of our output, not the time we spend.
  • We bring together legal, policy, and operational expertise in a single advisory model.
  • We partner with trusted experts in a range of other relevant fields, from economic analysis to behavioural psychology to information technology, to deliver holistic solutions
  • We tailor our approach to each client — no playbooks, no templated solutions.

Our Values

  • Independence — We give clear, unconflicted advice grounded in law, experience, and judgment.
  • Clarity — We simplify complexity without losing nuance.
  • Impact — We focus on what matters — the value of our output.
  • Discretion — We work quietly and carefully on high-trust matters.
  • Integrity — We believe in the rule of law and the importance of principled regulation.
