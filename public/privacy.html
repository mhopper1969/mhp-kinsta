What we collect

  • Contact details and correspondence when you email us or submit enquiries.
  • Business information you choose to share to help us understand your needs.
  • Technical logs generated by our hosting platform (security and performance).
  • Minimal, privacy-friendly usage analytics (see “Cookies & analytics”).
  • Newsletter details if you subscribe (email address, and if provided your name), plus consent and subscription preferences.

How we use your data (lawful bases)

  • Legitimate interests: responding to enquiries; operating, securing and improving our website; maintaining business records; marketing our own services to professional contacts.
  • Legal obligations: complying with applicable laws and regulatory requirements.
  • Consent: where you opt in to receive Insights and updates by email (you can withdraw at any time via the unsubscribe link or by contacting us).

Newsletter & updates

If you subscribe to our Insights and updates, we will process your email address (and, if you provide it, your name) to send you our emails. We use EmailOctopus as our email platform provider to create, send and manage these emails (including list management, deliverability and suppression of unsubscribed addresses). We use double opt-in and keep records of consent (who, when, how).

Our emails may include standard measurement technologies (e.g. open and click tracking) to help us understand reach and engagement. You can opt out of marketing at any time via the unsubscribe link in our emails. You can also reduce tracking by disabling images in your email client.

We retain your details while you remain subscribed. If you unsubscribe, we keep a minimal suppression record to ensure you do not receive further marketing emails.

We do not knowingly collect data from children and our mailing list is not intended for individuals under 16.

Sharing

We use trusted processors to operate our business infrastructure, including Microsoft 365 (email/document storage), our hosting provider (Kinsta), and EmailOctopus (email platform). We do not sell your personal data. We only share data where necessary for the purposes above or if required by law, under appropriate contractual safeguards.

International transfers

Your data is primarily processed in the UK/EU. EmailOctopus is UK-based but uses AWS infrastructure which may involve processing outside the UK/EEA. Where international transfers occur, we rely on an adequacy decision or on appropriate safeguards such as the UK Addendum to the EU Standard Contractual Clauses (and equivalent EU SCCs) to protect your information.

Retention

We keep enquiry correspondence and related records for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described above, typically up to six years, unless a longer period is required by law or to establish, exercise or defend legal rights. Newsletter data is retained while subscribed; suppression records are retained to respect opt-outs.

Your rights

You have rights under UK data protection law, including to access, correct, erase, restrict or object to processing, and data portability (where applicable). You can exercise these rights by contacting us. You also have the right to complain to the UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ico.org.uk).

Cookies & analytics

This site uses Kinsta’s privacy-friendly analytics and essential hosting logs to help us understand performance and detect abuse. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or cross-site tracking on our site.

If you use an EmailOctopus hosted landing page to subscribe, that page is provided by EmailOctopus and may use its own essential cookies and measurement technologies. Where we embed third-party scripts or forms on our domain, we will describe any non-essential cookies and provide controls where required.

You can use your browser settings to control cookies.
