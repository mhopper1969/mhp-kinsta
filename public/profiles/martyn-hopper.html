Martyn Hopper

Founder & Managing Director

Practising solicitor advising Boards and senior executives on regulatory exposure, governance and accountability — helping firms build systems and cultures that support sustainable growth.

Biography

Martyn is the founder of Martyn Hopper & Partners and a practising solicitor. He has over three decades’ experience in UK financial services regulation, including almost ten years at the Financial Services Authority. He then spent twenty-one years as a partner at leading City law firms Herbert Smith and Linklaters, advising global financial institutions and Boards on complex regulatory matters.

His practice focuses on senior management accountability, conduct and culture, and the design of clear, proportionate control frameworks. He helps clients navigate investigations and enforcement risk, and builds practical programmes — from Consumer Duty to governance and risk — that stand up to scrutiny while enabling predictable execution.

Areas of focus

Board counsel on accountability and culture; regulatory risk reviews and remediation; investigations and skilled-person engagements; Consumer Duty, governance and control-framework design; regulatory strategy and stakeholder engagement.

Career

Partner, Herbert Smith LLP (Financial Services Regulation) · Partner, Linklaters LLP (Financial Regulation & Enforcement) · Head of Enforcement Policy and Head of Market Abuse, Financial Services Authority (FSA).
