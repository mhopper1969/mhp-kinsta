Martyn Hopper

Founder and Managing Director

Former regulator and City law-firm partner. Three decades in the machinery of law, regulation and policy.

Biography

Martyn founded Martyn Hopper & Partners to bring together legal, regulatory and policy expertise in a way that helps organisations grow with integrity and clarity.

Originally called to the Bar by Middle Temple, he began his career in the Government Legal Service before joining the Financial Services Authority, where he held senior roles including Acting Head of Enforcement Law & Policy and Head of the Market Integrity Group in the Enforcement Division.

He later became a Partner in the Litigation Division of Herbert Smith LLP, specialising in contentious financial-services regulation, and subsequently a Partner — and National Practice Head — in the Financial Regulation Group at Linklaters LLP.

Across these roles, Martyn has worked at the intersection of law, regulation and public policy for more than thirty years — advising clients, firms and regulators on how effective regulation can support sustainable growth.
