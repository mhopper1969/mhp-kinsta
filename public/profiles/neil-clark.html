Biography

Neil Clark is the Founder and CEO of Integrated Human Factors (IHF) who are a global technology-enabled leader in all things human factors, working with some of the largest international companies across the world.

Providing skilled human factors practitioners to implement an array of consulting, training and leading-edge software and wearable technology solutions to identify and minimise the likelihood of human error and improve overall human performance.
