What We Do

We advise clients on regulatory strategy, compliance frameworks, governance, investigations, and the design of regulatory systems. Our work is grounded in legal expertise but always framed in the wider context of operational reality, reputational risk, and policy impact.

We don't apply templated solutions. We work closely with each client to understand their unique challenges and develop tailored strategies that deliver clarity, resilience, and credibility.

Who We Help

We work with:

  • Financial institutions
  • Corporates in regulated sectors
  • Start-ups navigating authorisation and growth
  • Regulators and public bodies designing or reforming systems
  • Individual senior executives facing regulatory scrutiny or transition

Our Services Include:

Regulatory Strategy

Helping clients understand, influence, and adapt to regulatory developments. We advise on long-term positioning, engagement with regulators, and aligning commercial goals with regulatory expectations.

Compliance Frameworks

Designing and evaluating systems of control, reporting, and oversight — from risk assessments to culture audits. We focus on proportionality, accountability, and effectiveness, not just box-ticking.

Regulatory Investigations

Guiding firms and individuals through regulatory inquiries, enforcement processes, and high-stakes reviews. Our approach combines legal rigour with strategic thinking and operational realism.

Governance and Culture

Advising boards and senior management on issues of tone, delegation, accountability, and decision-making. We help organisations build credible, transparent, and sustainable governance.

Policy and System Design

Supporting regulators and governmental bodies on the structure, goals, and enforcement of regulatory frameworks. Our work combines field-tested experience with leading academic thinking.

Research, Education and Training

We design and deliver bespoke education and training programmes that build regulatory and compliance capabilities within organisations. Our offerings include senior manager development on regulatory accountability, board-level education on emerging regulatory themes, compliance team training on risk assessment and culture, and industry workshops on regulatory reform and best practice.

We also conduct independent research on regulatory systems and compliance culture, contributing to policy debates and industry understanding through evidence-based analysis and practical insights. Whether you need to upskill your team, enhance board understanding, or contribute to industry knowledge, our services combine deep expertise with practical application.
